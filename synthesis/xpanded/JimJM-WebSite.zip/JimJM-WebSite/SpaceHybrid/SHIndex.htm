Space Hybrid

Space Hybrid or SH is a Role Playing Game I have authored (with the help of a large number of others) over the past 17 years.

Eventually, this page will contain all of my rules and design notes but for now I am simply posting the "flavor notes" or vignettes that I or others have put together to help set a mood or flavor for the game.
